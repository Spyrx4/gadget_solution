# STORE POLICIES & FAQ — GADGET SOLUTION

> This document contains official store policies and answers to common customer questions.
> Use this document to answer questions about shipping, returns, warranty, and payment.

---

## [POLICY-001] Shipping & Logistics

**Order Cut-off Time:**
Orders with confirmed payment before **15:00 WITA** will be processed and handed to the courier on the same day. Orders confirmed after that time will be processed the following day.

**Available Courier Partners:**
- JNE
- J&T Express
- SiCepat

**Shipping Insurance:**
All gadget purchases valued **above IDR 2,000,000** are required to use shipping insurance and additional wooden protective packaging. This is mandatory, not optional.

---

## [POLICY-002] Returns & Warranty Claims

**Return Claim Deadline:**
Return claims (unit replacement) may be submitted within a maximum of **2×24 hours** from when the delivery tracking status is marked "Delivered".

**Mandatory Return Requirements (all must be met):**

1. **Uninterrupted unboxing video** — The customer must provide a video of the unboxing from start (logistics package seal still intact) through to the device being powered on. The video must not be cut, paused, or edited in any way.
2. **Distributor seal intact** — The seal on the product box must not be damaged due to customer negligence.
3. **Not caused by human error** — Damage must not be caused by: dropping, water exposure, or software modification/rooting.

---

## [POLICY-003] Payment

**Q: Is installment payment available?**
Yes. Gadget Solution supports installment payments through:
- Credit card via **Midtrans Payment Gateway**
- Buy-now-pay-later: **GoPayLater**, **Kredivo**, **SpayLater**

**Q: Does the listed price include VAT?**
Yes. All prices shown in the catalog already include **Value Added Tax (VAT) at 11%**.
