## [FAQ-003] Shipping

**Q: How long does shipping take?**
Orders confirmed before 15:00 WITA are shipped the same day. Estimated delivery depends on the courier and destination (typically 1–3 days for Java and Kalimantan).

**Q: Can I choose my courier?**
Yes. JNE, J&T, and SiCepat are available. Select at checkout.

**Q: Why is shipping insurance required for my order?**
All gadgets above IDR 2,000,000 require shipping insurance and wooden protective packaging. This protects the product from damage in transit — the cost is borne by the buyer.

**Q: Does Gadget Solution ship outside Kalimantan?**
Yes. We ship nationwide through JNE, J&T, and SiCepat.

## [FAQ-004] Warranty & Returns

**Q: How long is the product warranty?**
Warranty follows the official distributor warranty for each brand. For ASUS ROG products, the official warranty is 2 years.

**Q: How do I claim a return if the product arrives damaged?**
Submit a claim within 2×24 hours of the tracking status showing "Delivered". Mandatory requirement: an uninterrupted unboxing video from the sealed package through to the device powering on.

**Q: Can I return a product damaged from dropping or water exposure?**
No. Damage caused by human error (dropping, water damage, software modification/rooting) is not covered by the return policy.
