# PRODUCT RECOMMENDATION GUIDE — GADGET SOLUTION

## [USECASE-001] Heavy AAA Gaming (PC/Laptop)

**Suitable for users who want to play:**
Black Myth: Wukong, Elden Ring, Cyberpunk 2077, or other demanding titles at High–Ultra graphics settings.

**Minimum System Requirements:**
- GPU: Equivalent to NVIDIA RTX 4060 or higher
- RAM: Minimum 16GB
- Storage: SSD required (Gen 4 recommended for fast load times)

**Recommended Product:**
→ **ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 (2025 Edition)** — SKU: ROG-G14-RTX4080-32GB
The RTX 4080 handles AAA games at 2K resolution without frame drops. Highly recommended for this use case.

---

## [USECASE-002] Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

**Suitable for users running:**
PyTorch, Transformer Model / local LLM training, or data science tooling.

**Minimum System Requirements:**
- GPU VRAM: Minimum 8GB, 12GB+ recommended (critical for Deep Learning, prevents Out of Memory errors during training)
- RAM: Minimum 32GB for large dataset preprocessing
- OS: Compatible with Python environment and Docker

**Recommended Product:**
→ **ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 (2025 Edition)** — SKU: ROG-G14-RTX4080-32GB
The RTX 4080 with 12GB VRAM and 32GB RAM makes it ideal for tensor computation and running Machine Learning models locally.

---

## [USECASE-003] Competitive Mobile Gaming

**Suitable for users who want to:**
Play competitive or demanding games on a smartphone during long sessions without overheating.

**Recommended Product:**
→ **ASUS ROG Phone 3** — SKU: ROG3-8-128-BLK
Strongly recommended to pair with the **AeroActive Cooler 3** accessory to keep the 6000mAh battery temperature stable during extended gaming sessions.
