## [COMPARE-001] ROG Phone 3 vs ROG Zephyrus G14 — Which to Choose?

Both are ASUS gaming products, but designed for very different needs.

| Aspect | ASUS ROG Phone 3 | ASUS ROG Zephyrus G14 (2025) |
|---|---|---|
| Price | IDR 9,999,000 | IDR 34,500,000 |
| Type | Gaming smartphone | Gaming/creator laptop |
| Processor | Snapdragon 865 Plus | AMD Ryzen 9 8945HS |
| GPU | Adreno 650 (mobile) | RTX 4080 12GB VRAM |
| RAM | 8GB | 32GB |
| Display | 6.59" AMOLED 144Hz | 14" OLED 3K 120Hz |
| Portability | Very high (smartphone) | High (slim laptop) |
| Best for | Competitive mobile gaming | AAA gaming, AI/ML, creators |

**Choose ROG Phone 3 if:** you want gaming anywhere from your hand, budget is limited, or you focus on mobile games (PUBG, Mobile Legends, Genshin Impact).

**Choose ROG Zephyrus G14 if:** you want to play demanding PC games (Cyberpunk, Elden Ring), work with AI/data science, or need a laptop that handles heavy multitasking.
